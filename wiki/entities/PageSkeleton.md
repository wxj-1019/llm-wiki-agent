[[PageSkeleton]] is a UI component from the LLM Wiki Viewer's `@/components/ui/Skeleton` module. In [[RootLayout]], it is displayed during the loading state (when `useWikiStore.loading` is true), providing a placeholder skeleton loader in place of page content.
