# QwQ

**QwQ** is an open-source reasoning model developed by Alibaba Cloud's Qwen team, released in late 2024 as part of the wave of "inference-scaling" models that followed the introduction of [[o1]] by [[OpenAI]]. Like [[DeepSeekR1]], QwQ implements a chain-of-thought reasoning process that scales inference-time compute to improve performance on complex mathematical, logical, and coding tasks. The model is notable for its strong benchmark results, often outperforming larger proprietary models on mathematics and reasoning benchmarks despite its relatively smaller size. QwQ is released under a permissive license, making it accessible for research and commercial applications. Its main significance in the 2024 LLM landscape is as evidence that the inference-scaling paradigm—previously exclusive to closed, proprietary systems like [[o1]]—can be successfully replicated by open-source initiatives, further accelerating the democratization of advanced reasoning capabilities.
