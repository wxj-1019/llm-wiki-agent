# DeepSeekV3

DeepSeekV3 is a large language model developed by the Chinese AI company DeepSeek, released in late 2024. Its primary significance lies in being a landmark open-weight model that broke the GPT-4 barrier—achieving performance comparable to or surpassing the original GPT-4 (from March 2023) while being dramatically cheaper to train and run. This was accomplished through a Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture that activates only a subset of its total parameters per token, enabling high efficiency and lower inference costs. DeepSeekV3's release represented a major milestone in the 2024 LLM landscape, as it demonstrated that GPT-4-class intelligence could be achieved with far fewer resources, accelerating the trend of falling prices and democratizing access to frontier capabilities. It is closely associated with the broader DeepSeek family (including the reasoning model DeepSeekR1) and exemplified the growing shift toward open-weight models that challenge proprietary leaders like OpenAI's GPT-4.
