# Google DeepMind

Google DeepMind is the AI research division of [[Google]]. In 2024, they released [[Gemini|Gemini 1.5 Pro]] (February 2024) with up to 2 million token context and video input — the first model to surpass the original [[GPT4]] on the Chatbot Arena. They also released [[Gemini|Gemini 1.5 Flash]] (ultra-cheap), [[Gemini2.0Flash]] (December 2024), and [[Gemini2.0FlashThinking]] — their entrant in the inference-scaling reasoning category.

## Key developments in 2024

- [[Gemini|Gemini 1.5 Pro]] — first GPT-4-beating model; 1M→2M token context; video input
- [[Gemini|Gemini 1.5 Flash]] — ultra-cheap multimodal model
- [[NotebookLM]] — audio summary tool with realistic podcast voices (September 2024)
- [[Gemini2.0Flash]] — December 2024
- [[Gemini2.0FlashThinking]] — inference-scaling reasoning model (December 2024)
- Live camera mode (December 2024)
