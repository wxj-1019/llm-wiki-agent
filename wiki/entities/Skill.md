# Skill

A **Skill** is a self-contained, reusable package of knowledge or capability that can be generated from within the [[LLMWikiViewer]]'s chat interface, specifically through the [[generateFromKnowledge]] function. It represents a structured output — distinct from freeform conversation — that captures, codifies, or automates a particular competency the system has acquired or been asked to assemble. Skills are generated alongside [[MCP]] packages via a dedicated "generate" button in the [[ChatInput]] toolbar, and they appear as a primary action in the generate-from-knowledge panel on the [[ChatPage]]. The term implies a portable artifact: once produced, a Skill can be stored, shared, or invoked, serving as a bridge between conversational AI interaction and persistent, actionable knowledge assets.
