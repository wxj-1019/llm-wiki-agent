# Ilya Sutskever

**Ilya Sutskever** is a co-founder and former Chief Scientist of [[OpenAI]]. He was previously a researcher at [[Google]] Brain, where he contributed to the sequence-to-sequence learning framework and early attention-based models that influenced the later development of the [[Transformer]] architecture.

At OpenAI, he led the research direction behind the [[GPT]] series, including [[GPT4]]. He left OpenAI in 2024 to found Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI).
