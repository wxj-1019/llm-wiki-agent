# GPT4

GPT-4 (Generative Pre-trained Transformer 4) is a large multimodal language model developed by [[OpenAI]], released in March 2023. In the context of this wiki, GPT-4 represents a significant advancement in the lineage of generative AI models, succeeding the initial [[ChatGPT]] release (which was based on GPT-3.5). Its primary significance lies in its substantially improved reasoning capabilities, factual accuracy, steerability, and ability to process both text and image inputs (multimodality), marking a qualitative leap from earlier models.

Unlike prior versions, GPT-4 demonstrates performance near or exceeding human-level benchmarks on numerous professional and academic exams. It is built on the [[Transformer]] architecture and is part of the broader [[GPT]] model family. GPT-4 serves as a reference point for comparing other [[LargeLanguageModels]], including open-source alternatives like [[LLaMA]].
