# GPT4

GPT-4 (Generative Pre-trained Transformer 4) is a large multimodal language model developed by OpenAI, released in March 2023. In the context of this wiki, GPT-4 represents a significant advancement in the lineage of generative AI models, succeeding GPT-3.5 (the engine behind the initial ChatGPT release). Its primary significance lies in its substantially improved reasoning capabilities, factual accuracy, steerability, and ability to process both text and image inputs (multimodality), marking a qualitative leap from earlier models. Unlike prior versions, GPT-4 demonstrates performance near or exceeding human-level benchmarks on numerous professional and academic exams (e.g., passing the Uniform Bar Exam in the top 10% of test-takers). Associated actions include its deployment in a wide range of applications—from coding assistants and content generation to complex problem-solving and data analysis—and its integration into products like ChatGPT Plus, Microsoft’s Copilot, and various enterprise APIs. Within the wiki, GPT-4 is often linked to discussions of advanced AI alignment, safety measures (such as reinforcement learning from human feedback, RLHF), and the evolving capabilities landscape of frontier models. It also serves as a reference point for comparing other large language models, including open-source alternatives.
