# ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a conversational AI model developed by [[OpenAI]], based on the Generative Pre-trained Transformer ([[GPT]]) architecture. In the context of this wiki, ChatGPT represents a landmark entity in the evolution of human-computer interaction, serving as both a widely accessible tool for natural language understanding and generation, and as a symbolic marker of the current era of [[LargeLanguageModels]]. Its main significance lies in its ability to engage in context-aware dialogue, answer questions, generate creative text, assist with programming, and perform a wide array of language-based tasks across diverse domains.

ChatGPT is associated with several key actions and attributes: it is primarily accessed through a web interface or API, often used for drafting, brainstorming, tutoring, and automating routine communication. Notably, later versions incorporate [[GPT4]] as their underlying engine, representing a significant leap in reasoning and multimodal capabilities. As an entity, ChatGPT is also emblematic of broader trends and debates regarding AI ethics, misinformation, job displacement, and the accessibility of advanced AI to the general public.
