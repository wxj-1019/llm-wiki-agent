# OpenAI

**OpenAI** is an artificial intelligence research laboratory founded in 2015 by [[SamAltman]], [[GregBrockman]], [[IlyaSutskever]], and others.

## Key Products
- [[ChatGPT]] — conversational AI based on [[GPT]] architecture
- [[GPT4]] — large multimodal language model
- DALL-E — text-to-image generation model

## Research Contributions
- Pioneered the scaling of [[Transformer]]-based models through the [[GPT]] series
- Demonstrated emergent capabilities in [[LargeLanguageModels]]
- Developed RLHF for alignment

## Connections
- [[Google]] — competitor in the LLM space
- [[Transformer]] — architecture used in their models
- [[AttentionMechanism]] — core technique

## Timeline
- 2015: Founded as non-profit
- 2018: GPT-1 released
- 2019: GPT-2 released
- 2020: GPT-3 released
- 2022: ChatGPT launched
- 2023: GPT-4 released
