[[useKeyboardShortcuts]] is a custom hook in the [[LLMWikiViewer]] frontend that registers global keyboard shortcut handlers. In [[RootLayout]], it is invoked at the root level (called twice in the source code) to enable application-wide keyboard shortcuts.
