**NotFoundPage** is the 404 fallback component for the [[LLMWikiViewer|LLM Wiki Viewer]]. It is rendered when no route matches the current URL.

- Defined in `@/components/pages/NotFoundPage`
- Used as the catch-all `'*'` route in [[router-configuration|Router Configuration]]
