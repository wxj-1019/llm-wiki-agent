# Simon Willison

Simon Willison is a prolific blogger, software developer, and creator of the [[Datasette]] ecosystem. He published the comprehensive review "Things we learned about LLMs in 2024" summarizing the year's developments. He is known for his LLM CLI tool ([[LLM]]), extensive blogging under the "llms" tag, and advocacy for practical, eval-driven LLM application development.

## Key Contributions

- Created the [[LLM]] CLI tool for interacting with multiple model providers
- Published annual LLM landscape reviews (2023, 2024)
- Developed [[Datasette]] for data exploration and publishing
- Known for his practical, grounded perspective on LLM capabilities and limitations
