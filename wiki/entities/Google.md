# Google

**Google** (now Alphabet) is a technology company that has made foundational contributions to artificial intelligence and machine learning.

## AI Research
- Published the original [[Transformer]] architecture (2017)
- Developed [[BERT]], a bidirectional encoder representation model
- Created [[T5]] and [[PaLM]] language models

## Key Teams
- Google Research
- DeepMind (acquired 2014)
- Google Brain (merged into DeepMind)

## Connections
- [[OpenAI]] — competitor; many OpenAI researchers formerly at Google
- [[Transformer]] — invented at Google Research
- [[AttentionMechanism]] — foundational work at Google
