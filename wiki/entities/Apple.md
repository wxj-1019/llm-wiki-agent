# Apple

Apple is a technology company whose AI initiatives in 2024 had a mixed reception. Their MLX library for Apple Silicon received high praise, enabling GPT-4 class models on Mac hardware. However, their [[AppleIntelligence]] features were described by [[SimonWillison]] as "bad" — offering notification summaries that misrepresent news and weak writing assistant tools. Genmoji were cited as a fun exception.

## Key developments in 2024

- MLX — excellent array framework for Apple Silicon with Python library
- [[AppleIntelligence]] — underwhelming LLM features (notification summaries, writing tools, Genmoji)
- [[MLXvlm]] — community project for vision LLMs on Apple Silicon

## Connections

- MLX — their ML library (excellent)
