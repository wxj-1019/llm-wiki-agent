# ChatHistoryItem

A component imported by [[ChatHistory]] that renders an individual session entry in the collapsible sidebar. It is used for display and receives callbacks: `onSwitch`, `onDelete`, `onRename`. The component is located at `code/wiki-viewer/src/components/chat/ChatHistoryItem`.
