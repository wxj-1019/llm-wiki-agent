# Dzmitry Bahdanau

**Dzmitry Bahdanau** is a machine learning researcher known for introducing the attention mechanism for recurrent neural networks (RNNs) in neural machine translation. His 2014 paper proposed "Bahdanau attention" (also known as additive attention), which allowed RNN-based encoder-decoder models to focus on relevant parts of the input sequence during translation.

The subsequent [[Attention Is All You Need]] paper by [[Vaswani]] et al. demonstrated that attention alone is sufficient, dispensing with recurrence entirely.
