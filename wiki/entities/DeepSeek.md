# DeepSeek

DeepSeek is a Chinese AI research lab that released [[DeepSeekV3]] on Christmas Day 2024 — a 685B parameter model trained for only ~$5.6M (2.78M H800 GPU hours), 11x less than [[LLaMA|Llama 3.1 405B]] yet benchmarking near [[Claude|Claude 3.5 Sonnet]]. They previously released [[DeepSeekR1-Lite-Preview]] (November 2024), a reasoning model used to generate training data for V3. The extreme cost efficiency is attributed to US export regulations spurring innovative training optimizations.

## Key developments in 2024

- [[DeepSeekR1-Lite-Preview]] — inference-scaling reasoning model (November 2024)
- [[DeepSeekV3]] — 685B parameter model, $5.6M training cost (December 2024)
- Highest-ranking openly licensed model on Chatbot Arena (7th place)
