# WikiIndex

The **Wiki Index** (`wiki/index.md`) is the central catalog of all wiki pages. It is updated on every successful ingest: new source pages, entity pages, concept pages, syntheses, and code pages are added under their respective sections. The index format is defined in the CLAUDE.md schema.
