# WikiOverview

The **Wiki Overview** (`wiki/overview.md`) is a living synthesis across all sources, manually maintained and updated by the [[IngestTool|ingest tool]] when a new source introduces significant cross-domain connections, contradictions, or shifts in the wiki's knowledge landscape.
