# Slop

"Slop" is a term that became a term of art in 2024 to describe unwanted AI-generated content, analogous to "spam" for unwanted email. Coined and popularized by Simon Willison, it refers specifically to AI-generated content that is both **unrequested** and **unreviewed**. It was in the running for Oxford Word of the Year 2024, but lost to "brain rot".

## Definition

> "Slop describes AI-generated content that is both unrequested and unreviewed." — Simon Willison

## Impact

- Gave society a concise way to talk about negative AI content
- Differentiates legitimate AI use from low-effort, low-quality generation
- Helps in AI literacy: "Ignore that article, it's slop"
