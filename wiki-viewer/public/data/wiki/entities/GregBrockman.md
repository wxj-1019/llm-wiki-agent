# Greg Brockman

**Greg Brockman** is a co-founder and former CTO of [[OpenAI]]. He was previously the CTO of Stripe before joining [[SamAltman]], [[IlyaSutskever]], and others to found OpenAI in 2015.

At OpenAI, he played a key role in scaling the engineering organization that built [[GPT]], [[ChatGPT]], and [[GPT4]].
