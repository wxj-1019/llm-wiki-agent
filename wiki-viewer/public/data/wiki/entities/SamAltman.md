# Sam Altman

**Sam Altman** is the CEO of [[OpenAI]], which he has led since 2019. Under his leadership, OpenAI launched [[ChatGPT]] (2022) and [[GPT4]] (2023), catalyzing the widespread adoption of [[LargeLanguageModels]].

Prior to OpenAI, he was the president of Y Combinator. He co-founded OpenAI alongside [[GregBrockman]], [[IlyaSutskever]], and others in 2015.
